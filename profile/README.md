# CLEAR Lab Open Source

CLEAR Lab is building an open-source space for robotics, embodied intelligence, simulation, control, and learning-based robot systems.

CLEAR Lab 正在建设一个面向机器人、具身智能、仿真、控制与学习型机器人系统的开源协作空间。

This page collects our public research code, teaching resources, reproducible experiments, and selected community collaborations.

这里汇总实验室公开的研究代码、教学资源、可复现实验，以及部分重点社区合作项目。

## Research and Project Areas / 研究与项目方向

- **Robotics control and learning**  
  机器人控制与学习

- **Embodied AI and Vision-Language-Action models**  
  具身智能与视觉-语言-动作模型

- **Simulation, evaluation, and reproducible experiments**  
  仿真、评测与可复现实验

- **Teaching, tutorials, and research engineering tools**  
  教学、教程与科研工程工具

## How to Participate / 如何参与

You can participate through issues, pull requests, documentation improvements, experiment reproduction, tutorials, and technical discussions.

你可以从 Issue、Pull Request、文档完善、实验复现、教程补充和技术讨论开始参与。

For project-specific questions, please use the issue tracker of the corresponding repository. For community collaboration or broader open-source discussion, contact us by email.

具体项目问题请优先在对应仓库提交 Issue。社区协作、项目共建或更广泛的开源交流，可以通过邮件联系我们。
